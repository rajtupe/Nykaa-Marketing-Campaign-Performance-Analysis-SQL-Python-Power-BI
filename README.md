This project implements an end-to-end marketing analytics pipeline to evaluate the performance of Nykaa's digital marketing campaigns using **SQL, Python, and Power BI**. The objective is to transform raw campaign data into actionable business intelligence by integrating data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business analytics, and interactive dashboard development.

The workflow begins with data cleaning and feature engineering, where missing values, duplicate records, and inconsistent data types are addressed to improve data quality. Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn) is used to perform exploratory analysis, identify distribution patterns, detect outliers, evaluate feature relationships, and derive marketing KPIs including Click-Through Rate (CTR), Lead Rate, Conversion Rate, Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Revenue, and Return on Investment (ROI).

Business-oriented analytical queries are implemented in SQL to answer critical marketing questions, such as campaign budget optimization, customer acquisition efficiency, audience segmentation, marketing funnel leakage, and high-performing combinations of campaign type, language, and target audience. Aggregations, filtering, grouping, ranking, and performance metrics are leveraged to generate actionable insights for decision-making.

The processed dataset is then visualized in Power BI through an interactive dashboard featuring KPI cards, drill-down reports, slicers, trend analysis, funnel visualization, channel performance comparisons, audience segmentation, and ROI analysis. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor campaign effectiveness, identify high-performing marketing channels, evaluate conversion performance, and optimize future marketing investments through data-driven decision-making.

⭐ Technical Highlights:

🔹 Developed a complete ETL and analytics workflow from raw data ingestion to interactive business intelligence reporting.
🔹 Performed data preprocessing, feature engineering, and exploratory data analysis using Python.
🔹 Solved real-world marketing business problems using advanced SQL queries involving aggregations, filtering, grouping, ranking, and KPI calculations.
🔹 Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with dynamic filters, DAX measures, KPI cards, and drill-through capabilities.
🔹 Analyzed campaign performance across marketing channels, audience segments, languages, customer acquisition cost, conversion funnel, revenue, and ROI.
🔹 Applied descriptive analytics to generate strategic recommendations for campaign optimization and marketing budget allocation.

This project demonstrates practical applications of **data analytics, business intelligence, marketing analytics, SQL querying, Python-based EDA, and dashboard development** to support evidence-based decision-making in digital marketing.
